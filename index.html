How It Works

1. Choose Your Apps: Select which apps and websites you want to block during focus time.

2. Set Your Timer: Pick your focus duration anywhere from 5 minutes to 3 hours.

3. Start Focusing: Your selected apps are blocked while the timer runs. Stay in the zone!

4. Earn Points: Complete your session and earn 10 points per minute. Build your streak!

5. Compete: Join leagues with friends and climb the leaderboard each week.
