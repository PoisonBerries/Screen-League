Transform Your Focus Into Achievement

Screen League is a focus timer app that helps you stay productive by blocking distracting apps during work sessions. Set your timer, pause distracting apps, and earn points for every completed focus session.

Compete in private leagues with friends, family, colleagues, or study partners. Turn productivity into a fun, social experience while building better focus habits.

Sync Leagues

Create multiplayer leagues synced via iCloud.

How It Works

1. Approve Blocking: Allow Screen Time access to pause distracting apps.

2. Set Your Timer: Pick a duration from 5 minutes to 3 hours.

3. Start Focusing: Selected apps are blocked while the timer runs.

4. Earn Points: Complete sessions and build your score.

5. Compete: Join leagues and climb the leaderboard.
